Rome

Rome, the capital city of Italy, is a timeless and captivating metropolis steeped in history and culture. Known as the "Eternal City," Rome's roots trace back over 2,000 years, making it one of the world's most iconic and historically significant destinations. It's renowned for its architectural marvels, including the Colosseum, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon, which offer glimpses into the grandeur of the Roman Empire.
